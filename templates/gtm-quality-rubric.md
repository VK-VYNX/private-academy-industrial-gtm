# GTM Quality Rubric

Score outputs from 1 to 5. Anything below 4 should be revised before use.

## Strategy Rubric

| Dimension | 1 | 3 | 5 |
|---|---|---|---|
| ICP | broad market | segment-level | narrow ICP with disqualification |
| Buyer committee | generic personas | roles listed | role concerns, proof, engagement mapped |
| Demand state | absent | mentioned | routing by content/solution/vendor demand |
| Channel strategy | random channels | channel list | channel matched to trust and demand state |
| RevOps | absent | metrics listed | fields, states, owners, SLA, dashboard |
| Buyer enablement | absent | collateral | champion and committee enablement pack |
| Measurement | vanity metrics | mixed metrics | account progression and pipeline quality |

## Account Research Rubric

| Dimension | Required For Score 5 |
|---|---|
| Fit | score and disqualification included |
| Trigger | evidence-based trigger hypothesis |
| Committee | roles, concerns, proof needed |
| Signal | source, demand state, strength |
| Next action | owner, SLA, artifact |
| Risk | blocker or false-positive risk |

## Content Rubric

| Dimension | Required For Score 5 |
|---|---|
| Buyer question | clear role and question |
| Demand state | content, solution, or vendor |
| Industrial specificity | application or process context |
| Expert input | SME or field insight |
| Distribution | owned, borrowed, sales, or partner path |
| Sales use | clear use in account progression |
