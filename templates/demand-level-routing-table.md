# Demand Level Routing Table

| Demand Level | Buyer Behavior | Content Needed | Sales Role | Wrong Move |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Content demand | learning about problem, trend, risk, process, compliance, efficiency | educational articles, expert posts, guides, research, webinars | observe, engage lightly, share helpful content | pitch product |
| Solution demand | comparing approaches, asking how to solve, using calculators, reading selection guides | frameworks, comparison guides, application notes, ROI logic, checklists | ask diagnostic questions, map committee, share proof | force demo before need is clear |
| Vendor demand | RFQ, spec review, audit, sample, consultation, site visit, pricing, technical evaluation | proof, case evidence, TCO, implementation, compliance, references | activate, qualify, multi-thread, enable champion | leave in nurture |
