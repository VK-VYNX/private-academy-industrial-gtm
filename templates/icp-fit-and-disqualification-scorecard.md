# ICP Fit And Disqualification Scorecard

Score accounts before assigning sales or ABM resources.

## Fit Score

| Dimension | Score 0 | Score 5 | Score 10 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Sector fit | outside priority sector | adjacent sector | priority sector |
| Application fit | unclear use case | possible use case | known use case |
| Geography | cannot serve | can serve with friction | strong service coverage |
| Project value | below minimum | acceptable | high value or repeat potential |
| Buying committee access | no known roles | one role known | three or more roles mapped |
| Trigger evidence | none | weak signal | clear trigger |
| Proof fit | no relevant proof | partial proof | strong matching proof |
| Channel path | unknown | possible route | direct, partner, or consultant route exists |

## Disqualification Rules

Disqualify if any are true:

- outside serviceable geography;
- order value below economics threshold;
- no realistic buying committee access;
- locked into long-term vendor with no trigger;
- not a real use case;
- cannot be served operationally;
- price-only buyer with no strategic fit;
- tender-only account with no influence path;
- distributor conflict cannot be resolved.

## Fit Bands

| Score | Decision |
|---|---|
| 70-80 | Tier 1 or Tier 2 candidate |
| 50-69 | Tier 2 or Tier 3 candidate |
| 35-49 | nurture only |
| below 35 | disqualify or ignore |
