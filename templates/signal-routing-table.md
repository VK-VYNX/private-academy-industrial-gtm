# Signal Routing Table

Use this to route account signals without confusing engagement with buying intent.

| Signal | Source | Demand State | Strength | If ICP Fit | If Not ICP Fit |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| single educational post engagement | social | content | weak | continue nurture | ignore |
| repeated engagement by same person | social/owned | content | moderate | add to research queue | low-touch nurture |
| multiple stakeholders engage | owned/social | solution | strong | review for Future Pipeline or Active Focus | qualify fit first |
| comparison guide visit | owned | solution | moderate | send related proof, map committee | no sales action |
| calculator or checklist use | owned | solution | moderate | progressive profiling | low-touch nurture |
| technical consultation request | owned | vendor | strong | sales action same day | qualify before sales |
| RFQ, spec, sample, audit, validation request | owned/sales | vendor | strong | opportunity review | qualify fit immediately |
| distributor flags project | partner | solution/vendor | strong if specific | sales validation | partner qualification |
| exhibition meeting with project detail | ecosystem | solution/vendor | strong | route within 72 hours | qualify fit |
| plant expansion signal | third-party | solution | moderate | account research | monitor |
| compliance audit window | third-party/sales | solution/vendor | strong | map quality role and activate | monitor |
| consultant asks technical question | ecosystem | solution/vendor | strong | sales and technical SME action | qualify account |
