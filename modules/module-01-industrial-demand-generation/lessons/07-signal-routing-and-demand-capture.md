# Unit 7: Signal Routing And Demand Capture

## Concept

The most expensive demand generation error is treating every signal as buying intent.

A signal has value only after it is interpreted through:

1. account fit;
2. demand state;
3. buying committee role;
4. evidence strength.

Only then should the team decide whether to nurture, research, map the committee, create an account point of view, activate sales, or open an opportunity review.

## Industrial Example

An engineer from a target account reads three technical articles. That is not automatically vendor demand.

The right route may be:

- classify as content or solution demand;
- verify account fit;
- identify the role;
- add to Future Pipeline;
- map the committee;
- send related proof;
- wait for stronger evidence before sales activation.

If the same account later requests a validation review or sends a spec, it becomes vendor-demand evidence and should trigger same-day sales action when fit is strong.

## Diagnostic Question

Which signals deserve education, research, activation, opportunity review, or disqualification?
