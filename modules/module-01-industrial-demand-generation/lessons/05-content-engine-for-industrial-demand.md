# Unit 5: Content Engine For Industrial Demand

## Concept

The content engine converts MOIN into buyer-useful assets that create trust, support sales, produce account-level signals, and reduce buying risk.

Industrial content should be built around:

- problem education;
- application education;
- technical decision criteria;
- comparison and selection guidance;
- compliance and validation;
- process economics;
- installation and implementation;
- maintenance and lifecycle;
- procurement and internal justification.

## Industrial Example

A transformer and electrical equipment supplier wants better project opportunities. Product brochures are not enough.

Useful content may include:

- loss and efficiency explainer for project owners;
- specification clarity guide for consultants and procurement;
- testing standards note for technical evaluators;
- service response proof for maintenance and plant teams;
- lifecycle-value model for finance and leadership.

## Diagnostic Question

Which content assets would make a buyer smarter, safer, and more able to move internally?

## Group Critique

For each asset, peers ask:

- Is it useful before the buyer is ready to talk to sales?
- Does it include expert insight or proof?
- Can sales use it without rewriting?
- Does it create a meaningful account signal?
- Is it mapped to the right demand state?

## AI-Assisted Attempt

Ask the AI agent to turn MOIN into a content plan.

Required AI output:

- asset list;
- demand state;
- buyer role;
- required SME input;
- sales use;
- signal generated;
- quality gate.

## Human Correction

Humans must remove:

- generic blog ideas;
- assets that are brochures too early in the journey;
- content that cannot be distributed;
- content that creates no useful signal;
- AI claims unsupported by evidence or SME input.

## Final Artifact

Content plan with at least:

- two content-demand assets;
- two solution-demand assets;
- one vendor-demand or buyer-enablement asset;
- owner and distribution path for each asset;
- signal design for each asset.
