# Implementation Checklist

Use this checklist when turning the module into a live client or internal pilot.

## Before The Pilot

- [ ] Revenue problem is defined.
- [ ] Category focus is narrow enough for 90 days.
- [ ] ICP and disqualification criteria are approved.
- [ ] Buying committee roles are mapped.
- [ ] Existing content is audited against MOIN.
- [ ] Priority proof gaps are identified.
- [ ] Distribution channels are selected by trust and stage.
- [ ] Sales, marketing, RevOps, leadership, and technical owner are named.
- [ ] Signal-routing table is approved.
- [ ] Weekly revenue cadence is scheduled.
- [ ] CRM fields or temporary tracking sheet can capture account, role, signal, state, owner, SLA, and next action.
- [ ] AI-agent prompts and quality gates are ready.

## During The Pilot

- [ ] Content is created from buyer questions, not campaign themes.
- [ ] SME or field insight is included in every major asset.
- [ ] Sales has a defined use for each content asset.
- [ ] Distribution happens through more than owned publishing.
- [ ] Weak, moderate, and strong signals are separated.
- [ ] Content demand, solution demand, and vendor demand are routed differently.
- [ ] Future Pipeline and Active Focus accounts have owners.
- [ ] Strong signals are reviewed within SLA.
- [ ] Buyer enablement assets are created for committee gaps.
- [ ] Weekly cadence reviews movement, stalls, proof gaps, and next actions.
- [ ] AI outputs are corrected before client or sales use.

## Weekly Inspection Questions

- [ ] Which target accounts moved?
- [ ] What caused movement?
- [ ] Which roles engaged?
- [ ] Which accounts are still single-threaded?
- [ ] Which signals were false positives?
- [ ] Which signals missed SLA?
- [ ] Which content assets created sales conversations?
- [ ] Which proof gaps are blocking movement?
- [ ] Which accounts should be disqualified or downgraded?
- [ ] What should we build, route, or repair next week?

## End Of Pilot

- [ ] Account movement is summarized.
- [ ] Committee coverage is inspected.
- [ ] Signal quality is reviewed.
- [ ] Opportunity quality is reviewed.
- [ ] Content and distribution performance are evaluated by account movement, not vanity metrics.
- [ ] Sales feedback is documented.
- [ ] Anti-pattern risks are reviewed.
- [ ] Benchmark assumptions are replaced with observed pilot data where possible.
- [ ] Executive readout recommends stop, repair, or scale.
