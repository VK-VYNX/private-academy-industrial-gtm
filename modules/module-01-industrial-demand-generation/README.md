# Module 01: Industrial Demand Generation

## Module Promise

This module trains humans and AI agents to design, diagnose, and operate an industrial demand generation engine that creates trust, account movement, and qualified pipeline in long-cycle manufacturing and export markets.

The module treats demand generation as an operating system:

1. define the revenue goal and market focus;
2. map ICP, buying committee, and demand states;
3. convert buyer questions into MOIN and content assets;
4. distribute insight through trusted industrial channels;
5. interpret signals without confusing learning intent with buying intent;
6. enable champions and buying committees;
7. run weekly cadence and 90-day pilot governance.

## Audience

- Agency employees who need to design and operate client demand systems.
- Client teams that need to collaborate across sales, marketing, RevOps, leadership, and technical functions.
- AI agents that need a governed workflow for industrial demand generation work.

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of the module, learners can:

- distinguish demand creation, distribution, capture, buyer enablement, and revenue cadence;
- define a narrow industrial ICP with disqualification logic;
- map buying committee roles, concerns, proof needs, and blockers;
- build a MOIN grid from buyer questions rather than campaign ideas;
- design content by demand state: content, solution, and vendor demand;
- build a distribution system across owned, expert-led, sales, partner, association, event, paid, and search channels;
- route signals by ICP fit, demand state, committee role, and evidence strength;
- define RevOps fields, owners, SLAs, dashboards, and weekly cadence questions;
- critique AI-generated GTM work before it reaches sales or clients;
- produce a 90-day Industrial Demand Generation Pilot.

## Capstone

The capstone is a 90-day Industrial Demand Generation Pilot for a selected manufacturing or export category. It must include:

- ICP definition;
- buying committee map;
- MOIN grid;
- content plan;
- distribution plan;
- signal-routing rules;
- weekly cadence;
- success metrics;
- anti-pattern risks;
- executive summary.

Use [capstone-project.md](capstone-project.md) and score it with [assessment-rubric.md](assessment-rubric.md).
