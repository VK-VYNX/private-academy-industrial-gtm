What The Extra Review Layers Mean

Confidentiality review means checking that no restricted real companies, clients, people, or earlier inspiration sources leaked into the academy.

Scoring calibration means checking whether reviewers would grade learner work consistently.

Pilot judgment means testing whether a real learner or team can use the module to build a 90-day industrial demand generation pilot.

Release decision means deciding whether this is official academy material, official with watch items, in repair, or on hold.
