## Format 3: Four Half-Day AI-Assisted Sprint

Best for: AI-enabled agency teams and internal operators.

| Session | Focus | AI Role | Human Correction |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | diagnostic, buyer reality, ICP | draft diagnosis and segment comparison | field reality and exclusions |
| 2 | MOIN and content engine | generate role questions and asset map | SME proof and sales use |
| 3 | distribution and signal routing | classify channels and signals | owner feasibility and false positives |
| 4 | cadence and capstone | assemble pilot and score risks | executive decision criteria |

Half-day sprint standard: AI accelerates drafts, humans own decisions.

## Format 4: Eight-Week Operating Cohort

Best for: client behavior change and implementation.

| Week | Session | Live Output | Between-Session Work |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | foundations | diagnostic | collect current assets |
| 2 | buyer reality | committee map | validate with sales |
| 3 | ICP and focus | ICP and exclusions | build target account list |
| 4 | MOIN | buyer question grid | interview sales or SME |
| 5 | content engine | asset plan | draft first assets |
| 6 | distribution | channel plan | launch first distribution actions |
| 7 | signal routing | routing table and SLA | configure tracking |
| 8 | cadence and pilot | executive-ready 90-day plan | begin pilot |

Eight-week standard: the pilot should start immediately after week 8.
