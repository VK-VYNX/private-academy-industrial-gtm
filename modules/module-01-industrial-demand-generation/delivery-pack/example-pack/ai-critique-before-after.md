# AI Critique Before And After

## Scenario

Category: specialty process chemicals for manufacturers with process stability and documentation requirements.

Task: create content ideas for demand generation.

## Weak AI Output

| Suggestion | Why It Fails |
|---|---|
| "Top 10 benefits of our chemicals" | product-first and not mapped to buyer uncertainty |
| "Why choose us as your supplier" | vendor demand too early |
| "The future of chemicals in manufacturing" | broad and not useful to sales |
| "Download our brochure" | weak buyer education and poor signal quality |

## Human Correction Questions

- Which buyer role has the process stability concern?
- What content-demand question exists before vendor evaluation?
- What solution-demand question helps the buyer compare approaches?
- What proof would sales need after engagement?
- What signal would justify a next action?

## Repaired Output

| Asset | Buyer Question | Demand State | Sales Use | Signal |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| process instability troubleshooting guide | why are batch results varying? | content | opens diagnostic conversation with production and process roles | target account engages with troubleshooting topic |
| formulation change risk checklist | what should be tested before changing inputs? | solution | supports consultation and sample planning | checklist use by technical or quality role |
| documentation readiness sheet | what documentation reduces audit and procurement risk? | vendor | helps champion involve quality and procurement | request for documentation review |

## Lesson

AI speed is useful only when humans enforce:

- industrial specificity;
- role-specific buyer questions;
- demand-state discipline;
- sales use;
- signal design;
- proof requirements;
- confidentiality boundaries.
