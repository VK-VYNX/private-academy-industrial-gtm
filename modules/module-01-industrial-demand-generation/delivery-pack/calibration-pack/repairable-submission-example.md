# Repairable Submission Example

This is an anonymized composite example. It has useful thinking but needs repair before field use.

## Category

Export-oriented manufacturers of engineered pump skids for process plants in harsh operating environments.

## Executive Summary

The pilot will focus on accounts that are planning plant expansions or replacing aging fluid-handling infrastructure. The program will use technical content to educate engineering and operations teams, then route stronger buying signals to sales.

## ICP

Target accounts:

- process manufacturers with multi-site operations;
- export or cross-border procurement complexity;
- active expansion, replacement, or reliability initiative;
- high downtime cost.

Disqualify accounts when:

- they only need commodity replacement parts;
- no technical owner is visible;
- project value is below the threshold for consultative sales involvement.

## Buying Committee

| Role | Concern | Proof Need |
|---|---|---|
| plant engineering | fit and reliability | application notes and drawings |
| operations | downtime and maintainability | service and failure-mode guidance |
| procurement | supplier risk | qualification checklist |
| finance | payback | cost-of-downtime model |

## MOIN

| Demand State | Question | Asset |
|---|---|---|
| content demand | Why are failures increasing? | diagnostic guide |
| solution demand | What design approaches reduce downtime? | comparison guide |
| vendor demand | What proof do we need to shortlist a supplier? | qualification checklist |

## Content Plan

The plan includes a diagnostic guide, a design-comparison article, a webinar with an engineer, and a supplier qualification checklist.

## Distribution Plan

The plan uses owned email, sales sharing, association newsletters, partner referrals, and event follow-up.

## Signal Routing

Strong signals include repeat visits to the qualification checklist, direct questions about integration, and sales conversations with engineering or operations.

## Weekly Cadence

The team will review account movement, content engagement, sales feedback, and upcoming activation tasks.
