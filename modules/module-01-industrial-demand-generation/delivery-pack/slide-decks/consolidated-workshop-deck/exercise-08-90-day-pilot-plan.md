# Exercise 08 - 90-Day Pilot Plan Worksheet

Use this worksheet with Slide 34 of the Industrial Demand Generation workshop.

This exercise assembles your prior workshop outputs into a 90-day Industrial Demand Generation pilot. The goal is not to write a long strategy document from scratch. The goal is to produce a clear pilot that leadership can approve, repair, or stop from evidence.

## Exercise Outcome

By the end of this exercise, you will have:

- Final pilot scope;
- Artifact readiness check;
- 90-day phase plan;
- Weekly revenue cadence;
- Success measures;
- Pilot risks and repair actions;
- Stop, Repair, Scale criteria;
- Executive summary;
- Next 30-day commitment.

The output becomes the final 90-Day Industrial Demand Generation Pilot Plan in your workshop strategy.

## How to Complete This Exercise

1. Check whether the prior artifacts are ready or need repair.
2. Select five to seven success measures from the Success Measure Menu.
3. Select five or six cadence items from the Weekly Cadence Menu.
4. Fill the fixed 90-day phase plan.
5. Select the most likely pilot risks and repair actions.
6. Define Stop, Repair, and Scale criteria.
7. Draft the executive summary.
8. Assemble the final pilot plan.

Keep the pilot narrow enough to learn in 90 days. Do not use vanity metrics as primary proof.

## Success Measure Menu

Select five to seven measures only.

| Measure | Use When |
|---|---|
| Target Accounts Reached | The pilot needs proof of relevant reach. |
| High-Fit Accounts Engaged | Account fit matters more than raw traffic. |
| Repeat Engagement From Target Accounts | Learning may be turning into account-level interest. |
| Buying Roles Identified | Committee visibility is improving. |
| Proof Gaps Closed | Buyer risk is being reduced. |
| First-Five Assets Completed or Repaired | The asset system is becoming usable. |
| Trusted Distribution Cadence Maintained | Content is circulating through real channels. |
| Signals Routed With Action Timing | Demand capture is becoming operational. |
| Sales or Technical Expert Actions Completed | Routed signals are creating useful action. |
| Qualified Opportunity Reviews Created | Strong evidence is reaching commercial review. |
| Low-Fit Accounts Disqualified | The system is protecting effort. |

## Weekly Cadence Menu

| Cadence Item | Decision to Make |

## Pilot Phase Plan

Use fixed phases to avoid blank-page planning.

| Phase | Days | Required Focus | Owner | Required Output |
|---|---:|---|---|---|
| Setup | 1-15 | Focus, committee map, tracking fields, cadence setup |  | Operating Baseline |
| Build and Distribute | 16-45 | First-five assets, trusted distribution paths, signal capture |  | Asset and Channel System |
| Route and Learn | 46-75 | Signal routing, sales use, proof repair, account movement review |  | Evidence Loop |
| Decide | 76-90 | Sponsor review, learning summary, Stop / Repair / Scale decision |  | Executive Decision |

## Pilot Risk and Repair Menu

| Risk | Repair Action |
|---|---|
| Focus Too Broad | Narrow the segment, application, trigger, or account list. |
| Weak Proof | Repair proof asset, add technical validation, or pause unsupported claim. |
| Committee Invisible | Map roles, enable champion, or add role-specific asset. |
| Distribution Weak | Shift to sales, partner, event, association, or expert-led path. |
| Signal Noise | Tighten fit, role, demand state, and strength rules. |
| Sales Not Using Assets | Assign sales use case, owner, and weekly inspection. |
| Revenue Operations Blind Spot | Add working board, field, owner, or review date. |
| Cadence Becomes Reporting | Force every agenda item to produce a decision and owner. |
| Premature Scale Pressure | Hold scale until movement, signal quality, and sales use are visible. |

## Stop, Repair, Scale Menu

| Decision | Use When |
|---|---|
| Stop | Focus is weak, proof is missing, access is poor, sales cannot act, or signals are low-fit. |
| Repair | Evidence exists, but one operating layer is weak or inconsistent. |
| Scale | Account movement, signal quality, sales use, proof coverage, and cadence evidence are visible. |
