## Choose From These Options

Use this scoring ladder for every layer.

| Score | Meaning | Choose This When |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Not Clear | The team does not have a real answer yet. |
| 2 | Weak | The answer exists, but it is vague or inconsistent. |
| 3 | Basic | The layer exists, but it does not reliably guide action. |
| 4 | Usable | The layer is clear enough to support a 90-day pilot. |
| 5 | Strong | The layer is clear, used by the team, and supported by evidence. |

Score these six layers.

| Layer | What You Are Checking |
|---|---|
| Market Focus | Do we know which market or segment we are pursuing? |
| Ideal Customer Profile | Do we know which accounts are worth focused effort? |
| Buyer Education and Proof | Do buyers have useful answers and credible proof? |
| Trusted Distribution | Can our ideas reach buyers through paths they trust? |
| Signal Meaning and Next Action | Do we know what buyer actions mean and what should happen next? |
| Weekly Review and Ownership | Does the team review movement and make decisions every week? |
