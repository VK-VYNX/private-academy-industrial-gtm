## Choose From These Options

Pick the roles that matter for your product or category. Use five to seven roles.

| Role Option | Use When |
|---|---|
| Champion | One person may push the idea internally. |
| Decision Maker | Someone can approve, reject, or delay the purchase. |
| Technical Evaluator | Engineering, application, specification, or validation matters. |
| Production or Operations | Uptime, throughput, disruption, or adoption matters. |
| Quality or Compliance | Inspection, audit, documentation, or validation matters. |
| Maintenance or Service | Serviceability, spares, lifecycle, or support matters. |
| Procurement | Supplier comparison, price, terms, or onboarding matters. |
| Finance | Payback, budget, Total Cost of Ownership (TCO), or Return on Investment (ROI) matters. |
| Leadership | Strategic risk, growth, reputation, or capital approval matters. |
| Distributor, Consultant, or Advisor | External influence shapes trust or access. |

Choose plain-language options for each role.

| Field | Example Options |
|---|---|
| What They Care About | uptime, quality, cost, safety, delivery, support, approval, risk reduction |
| What Could Block Them | unclear proof, disruption risk, weak payback, supplier doubt, missing documentation |
| Proof Needed | application example, technical note, checklist, payback logic, service plan, internal summary |
