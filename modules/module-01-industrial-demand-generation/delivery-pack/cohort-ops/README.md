## Recommended Pilot Cohort

Run the first academy-grade test with:

- 2 sales participants;
- 1 marketing participant;
- 1 RevOps participant;
- 1 technical or SME participant;
- 1 facilitator;
- 1 AI operator or AI-agent reviewer.

The pilot should produce at least one complete capstone and at least three scored unit artifacts.

## Cohort Success Standard

The cohort succeeds when learners can explain and use:

- demand-state separation;
- ICP and disqualification;
- buying committee proof needs;
- MOIN;
- content-to-distribution-to-signal logic;
- signal routing with owner and SLA;
- weekly cadence and success metrics.

If learners enjoy the module but cannot produce those artifacts, the cohort is not a success yet.
