# Certification Board

Use this meeting format to decide whether learner capstones and Module 1 releases are ready.

## Participants

- facilitator;
- sales reviewer;
- marketing reviewer;
- RevOps reviewer;
- technical or SME reviewer when available;
- AI operator when AI was used;
- academy owner for release decisions.

## Board Inputs

| Input | Required |
|---|---|
| learner capstone | yes |
| assessment rubric score | yes |
| quality gates checklist | yes |
| AI correction log when AI was used | yes |
| cohort feedback log | for cohort decisions |
| validation report | for module release decisions |
| visual render QA | for module release decisions |

## Decision Sequence

1. Confirm confidentiality boundary.
2. Confirm ICP and disqualification.
3. Confirm buying committee and proof needs.
4. Confirm MOIN quality.
5. Confirm content-to-distribution-to-signal logic.
6. Confirm RevOps fields, owner, SLA, dashboard, and cadence.
7. Confirm anti-pattern risk controls.
8. Confirm AI output was corrected by a human.
9. Decide pass, repair, reject, or hold.

## Decision Rules

| Decision | Standard |
|---|---|
| pass | ready for supervised implementation |
| repair | useful but missing evidence, owner, routing, cadence, or specificity |
| reject | generic, unsafe, confidential, or non-operational |
| hold | cannot decide until a critical unknown is resolved |
