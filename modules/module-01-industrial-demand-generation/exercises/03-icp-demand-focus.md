# Exercise 03: ICP And Demand Focus

## Purpose

Narrow the market for a 90-day pilot and prevent broad, unfocused demand generation.

## Inputs

- Candidate sectors or account clusters.
- Deal value or project value range.
- Sales cycle and buying process.
- Known triggers and disqualification factors.

## Instructions

1. Compare three candidate segments.
2. Score each segment by fit, demand evidence, access, and value.
3. Write the ICP.
4. Write explicit disqualification rules.
5. Select one 90-day pilot focus.

## ICP Statement

```text
We will focus on [account type] in [sector/geography] with [trigger] because [operational problem] creates demand for [outcome]. The buying committee usually includes [roles]. We will exclude [rules].
```

## Critique Prompts

- Is the ICP narrow enough for 90 days?
- What is the strongest trigger?
- What account should sales stop chasing?
- What evidence would move an account from Cluster ICP to Future Pipeline?

## AI Prompt

```text
Compare these candidate industrial segments for a 90-day demand generation pilot. Score fit, demand evidence, access path, value potential, disqualification risk, and recommended focus. Avoid broad TAM logic. Use account movement logic.
```
