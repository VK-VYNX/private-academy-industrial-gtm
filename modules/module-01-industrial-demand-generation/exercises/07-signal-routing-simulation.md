# Exercise 07: Signal Routing Simulation

## Purpose

Practice routing signals without confusing learning intent and buying intent.

## Inputs

- ICP definition.
- Content and distribution plan.
- Sample signals from owned, social, sales, third-party, event, or partner sources.

## Instructions

1. Classify each signal by source.
2. Check account fit.
3. Identify buying committee role.
4. Assign demand state and strength.
5. Define owner, SLA, next action, and false-positive risk.
